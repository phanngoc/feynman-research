## Cross-verification notes

1. **600K IT workforce figure:** Confirmed independently by TopDev 2023, JT1 2026, Aniday 2024 — consistent
2. **150K–200K shortage/year:** TopDev 2023 source; Nhân Dân cites 700K need vs 500K supply (similar gap)
3. **ITviec Q2/Q3 data:** Platform-internal data with noted caveats (posting volume ≠ hiring intent)
4. **Salary figures:** ITviec reports VND 43.7M average; VIR translates to ~$1,820/month — consistent at ~24,000 VND/USD
5. **Digital economy GDP contribution:** Two conflicting 2025 figures — Tổng cục Thống kê says 14% ($72.1B), while VnEconomy cited 18.3% for 2024. Discrepancy likely due to different measurement methodologies (narrow ICT vs broad digital economy). Report notes both.
6. **Semiconductor $18.7B revenue:** ITPC figure includes FDI firms' total output, not just domestic value-added. Flagged in report.
7. **ILO 11.5M workers affected by GenAI:** Specifically "potentially exposed," not "will lose jobs" — nuance preserved in report.

## Gaps and limitations

- TopDev and ITviec full PDF reports are gated — relied on public summaries and excerpts
- Long-range projections (2030–2035) from market research firms carry significant uncertainty
- VietnamWorks data referenced indirectly through VietData analysis, not primary access
- Salary data varies by source methodology (self-reported surveys vs platform data vs recruiter estimates)
- No direct access to McKinsey Vietnam-specific reports (used global findings + Vietnam extrapolation)
